Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on the health management system?

To register, click on the "Register" link in the navigation menu. Fill in the required details including your name, email, and contact number. Once registered, you'll receive your student ID which you can use for all health services.

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

To book an appointment, navigate to the "Book Appointment" page. Select your preferred doctor, date, and time slot. Enter the reason for your visit and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation message upon successful booking.

How do I access my health records?

You can access your health records by visiting the "Health Records" page and entering your student ID. The system will display your complete medical history including past consultations, diagnoses, and treatments.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, you can use the "Emergency Alerts" feature to notify the health center immediately. Alternatively, you can call the emergency contact number: +91-712-2801234. The health center operates 24/7 for emergencies.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment by contacting the health center directly. Please do so at least 24 hours in advance to allow other students to book the slot.

Are specialist consultations available at the health center?

Yes, the health center offers specialist consultations including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, and psychiatry. Check the "Doctor Availability" page to see when specialists are available.

Is there any cost for medical services at the health center?

Basic medical services are provided free of cost to all registered students. However, certain specialized tests or treatments may have nominal charges. The health center staff will inform you about any applicable charges before proceeding with such services.
